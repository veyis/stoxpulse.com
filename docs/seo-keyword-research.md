## Featured Snippet Strategy

**27% of finance keywords trigger featured snippets.** Here is how to capture them:

1. **Format definitions in 40-50 words** as the opening paragraph
2. **Use H2/H3 headings as questions** matching PAA queries
3. **Include comparison tables** (e.g., 10-K vs 10-Q, EPS vs Revenue)
4. **Use numbered lists** for "how to" content
5. **Add structured data** (FAQ schema, HowTo schema)
